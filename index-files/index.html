Build your own character!

This site is designed to make it super simple for anyone to build a D&D character! Whether you're a complete beginner, or you're a seasoned player, this site makes it super easy for you to build your next Dungeons and Dragons character.

From this page, you'll be able to find everything you need to make your very own D&D character! Simply navigate to the Races page to find all of the various races you can play and their benefits. Afterwards, head on over to the Classes page and pick which one sounds most fun to you. Remember, there are no wrong choices!

Then, once you have chosen your race and class, you're ready to make your character! You can find the character builder on the builder page, where you can roll stats, choose feats, and get going with your Dungeons and Dragons adventures. Happy rolling!
